Phase 48: Adaptive Research Planning

This commit introduces the Adaptive Research Planning and Backlog layer.
It implements the `ResearchTaskRegistry`, `PlanningSignalCollector`, `BacklogBuilder`, `PriorityScoring`, and `NextBestExperiment` recommendation engines.
It also includes reports for `RoadmapHealth` and `ResearchDebt` to aid in orchestrating offline tasks.

No live trading instructions, broker orders, or auto-deployments are included.
