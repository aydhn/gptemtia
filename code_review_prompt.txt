Review the following changes related to Phase 30: ML Training Baseline Models and Evaluation Layer.

- `ml/training_config.py` and `ml/model_labels.py`
- `ml/preprocessing.py` and `ml/baseline_models.py`
- `ml/model_schema.py`
- `ml/cross_validation.py` and `ml/model_evaluator.py`
- `ml/model_trainer.py`
- `ml/model_artifacts.py` and `ml/model_registry.py`
- `ml/model_quality.py` and `ml/training_pipeline.py`

- `data/storage/data_lake.py` and `reports/report_builder.py` integration

Please review for modularity, safety (no live trading language), and proper structure.
